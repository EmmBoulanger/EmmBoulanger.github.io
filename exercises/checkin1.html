Peanut Butter Blossom Cookie Recipe

Yes, that is what they are called, the peanut butter sugar cookie with the hershey's kiss on top has an actual name. Perfect for a sweet treat anytime of the year. This recipe will guide you step by step how to bake these delicious cookies for your next family gathering.

The first thing to do is gather the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar, plus extra for rolling
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 36-40 unwrapped Hershey's Kisses

You will also need 2 large bowls and 2 medium sized baking sheets, for reference, here is a baking sheet I highly recommend.

Once you have all of your ingredients its time to start baking!

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF.
  2. Line two medium size baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg, milk, and vanilla extract to the bowl.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients until combined.
  7. Shape dough into 1-inch marshmellow shapes and roll them in the extra granulated sugar.
  8. Place 2-inches apart on the prepared baking sheets.
  9. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until teh edges are set and the tops are slightly cracked.
  10. Remove from the oven and immediately press a hershey's kiss into the center of each cookie.
  11. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then move to a wire rack to finish cooling completely.

A little something me and my family like to do, once the hershey's kisses are pressed on, wait for the chocolate to melt a little. Then, with a spoon or your finger, gently tap the top of the kiss to flatten it. This helps you not stab yourself biting into the cookie!

And that's it! It's time to enjoy a fan favorite cookie recipe with everyone (or just for yourself)!
